Grooming

It is important to keep your poodle terrier
groomed regularly. You should get your dog
groomed every six to eight weeks to keep
them healthy and clean. You may choose
to wait a few extra weeks if you like them to
be longer especially during the cold, winter
months. You may choose to groom your dog
yourself by purchasing a grooming kit which
you can pick up at a local pet store or order
from Amazon. You can also choose to take him/her
to a groomer, PetCo, PetSmart or get a mobile
groomer to come to your house and cut them.
